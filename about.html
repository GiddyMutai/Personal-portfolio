ABOUT ME

I am born and bred in Eldoret, Kenya. I schooled there for most of my early years, before going to high school in Western, Kenya. I went to the Friends School Kamusinga and upon graduation, I got admission to Kenyatta University. I've always had an interest in pursuing Software Engineering, and have ambitions of becoming a full-stack developer. I am always intrigued with the UI/UX of most applications and website interfaces, but at the same time interested in getting to know how all of that interacts at the back-end. Therefore, when the opportunity to join Moringa School presented itself, I took it, and I hope the journey will be worthwhile.
